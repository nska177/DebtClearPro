DebtClearPro Tutorial
Welcome to DebtClearPro!
  1. Enter Your Financial Details: Input your monthly income, expenses, SIP contributions, and any extra payments you can make towards your debts.
  2. Add Your Debts: Click on "+ Add Another Debt" to input each of your debts, specifying the type, interest rate, balance, and minimum payment.
  3. Select a Repayment Method: Choose from Snowball, Avalanche, Pro-Rata, or Equal Distribution to determine how your payments are allocated.
  4. View Your Plan: Your repayment schedule, total interest paid, and a dynamic chart will update in real-time as you adjust your inputs.
  5. Track Achievements: Unlock badges as you add debts, clear debts, and reach financial milestones!

Feel free to explore and adjust your inputs to see how different strategies can help you achieve financial peace.

Choose a Repayment Method

Pick a method that resonates with you:

  • Snowball: Start with your smallest debt first. Quick wins build momentum.
  • Avalanche: Target highest interest debt first. Saves more money in the long run.
  • Pro-Rata: Distribute leftover funds proportionally. Balanced and fair.
  • Equal Distribution: Spread leftover funds equally among all debts. Simple and even-handed.

Month 0 shows your start. Each following month details your payments per debt. Over time, watch your debts fade away with consistent effort, like adding daily drops of water to a seedling until it blooms.
